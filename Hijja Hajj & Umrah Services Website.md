## **Project: Hijjah Hajj & Umrah Services Website** 

You are a senior product designer, UX designer, motion designer, and full-stack engineer. 

Your task is to design and build a world-class Hajj & Umrah booking platform for **Hijjah Hajj & Umrah Services** . 

## **Vision** 

This is **not** a travel agency website. 

This is a digital experience that prepares Muslims for one of the most important journeys of their lives. 

The website should feel: 

- Peaceful • Elegant 

- Premium 

- Spiritual 

- Modern 

- Luxurious 

- Emotional 

- Trustworthy 

Imagine combining the visual quality of Apple, the simplicity of Airbnb, the storytelling of National Geographic, and the hospitality of a luxury hotel—while maintaining deep respect for the sacred nature of Hajj and Umrah. 

Avoid anything flashy, noisy, or gimmicky.
